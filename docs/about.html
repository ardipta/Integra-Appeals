Our VISION

Our vision is to achieve 100% customer satisfaction by delivering quality products at an affordable cost. Our forward vision is to strive to become an entity in RMG sector. We are growing and would always like to remain on the growing streak.

The most unique part of our company is its management, which happens be a team of skilled, well experienced technical & managerial persons.

Quality ASSURANCE

Quality is the outcome of relentless effort of the management and the workers as well. Our quality control department works directly under the supervision of top Management.

Quality Assurance Department cheeks and run at every stage of production starting from cutting to the finishing. Now, we are installing Traffic Light system in our production process.

Our MISSION

Our mission is to achieve the reputation of a quality, high standard & reliable readymade garment manufacturer in the RMG trade.

We believe in the following core values in serving our clients: Honesty, Creativity, Quality, Professionalism, Commitment, Knowledge & Skill

COMPLIANCE AND CERTIFICATES

Integra Apparels (Bangladesh) Ltd. has already achieved ACCORD, BSCI, SEDEX, GOTS, OCS, OEKO-TEX, ISO etc. certificate that fully Compliant with Local Laws, International Labor Laws and many Buyers’ Requirements.
